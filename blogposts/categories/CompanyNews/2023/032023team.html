Meet Our Design Team Leader, Hussain!

Tsunoda Stylings is a start-up company that strives to create successful, responsive websites that are fast, easy to use, and built with the best techniques. At Tsunoda Stylings, we search for new talent to develop their portfolios via paid-per-project remote work! Not too long after we opened our doors, we received a request from an experienced designer and marketing expert looking to enhance his skills.

Meet Hussain Ashar, is a Maldives native who has a degree in Multimedia and Graphic Design. Read more below to learn more about him and what he plans to accomplish during his time here at Tsunoda Stylings.

Interview with Hussain Ashar:

Jasmine: "Hello Hussain, could you please introduce yourself to the readers?"

Hussain: "I’m Hussain Ashar from Maldives. I love designing and art. "

Jasmine: "When did you start applying for design and marketing jobs?"

Hussain: "Since 2015, I’ve been working in different design and marketing jobs."

Jasmine: "Why did you decide to start studying design and marketing?"

Hussain: "After I graduated from high school, I started with my degree Multimedia and Graphic Design in 2011 in Malaysia."

Jasmine: "What made you apply for the job opening at Tsunoda Stylings, and how do you like it so far?"

Hussain: "Because the prospect of working abroad was appealing to me because I would be exposed to different cultures and style of design that I may not have exposure to in my country."

Jasmine: "So, what do you like most about being a designer and marketing manager?"

Hussain: "I like the the ability to express my creativity in so many ways. And the fact that anything is possible in the realm of design."

Jasmine: "What do you find most challenging about being a designer and marketing manager?"

Hussain: "Trying to make each design and creative output 100% perfect, even if they already meet the specified requirements. Sometimes its difficult to put down my work because I feel like there is always something more I could add."

Jasmine: "What do you hope to accomplish while working with us?"

Hussain: "Growth in every aspect of design and marketing! There is always LOADS to learn and I’m happy I’ve been given as many opportunities in the past and I’m hoping to keep going down that path for as long as I’m working."

Jasmine: "What advice would you give to anyone wanting to become a web developer/designer or social media marketer?"

Hussain: "My Advice would be to Don’t do it all at once, take your time and select your goals accordingly. Slowly work on improving each skillset one at a time and once you have the fundamentals down, make sure to continuously practice these skills as often as possible. Also Design and Marketing Strategies change often as the world is constantly evolving so pay attention to changing trends."

Whether you are a student looking for a major to study in, wanting to learn a new skill for a promotion, or a professional considering a career change, Hussain has shown us that it is never too late to pursue your dreams with a bit of determination.
