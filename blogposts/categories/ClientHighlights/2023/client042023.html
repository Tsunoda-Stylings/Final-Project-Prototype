All About Ebony J Media:

Ms. Ebony J. is an experienced media strategist. She has helped companies across America increase sales, lead generation, and customer retention through social media marketing for both business owners & nonprofit organizations.

Here’s How It All Started…

Ebony J started at the small radio station on NKU's (Northern Kentucky University) online campus back when social media wasn't too popular, and people used email or text messages to communicate with friends. She saw how valuable it could be for marketing.

Please meet Jeffonia Wynn, aka Ms. Ebony J. She is the owner of Ebony J Media LLC, a multiple-business empire that has taken America by storm! Read below to learn more about her, and stay tuned later in the year for her video interview with Tsunoda Stylings!

Seeing it as a potential opportunity, she started attracting more people to listen to her radio show. Her show surpassed 1,000 online listeners by 2012 and didn't have enough space to host the crowd. Two years later, in 2014, she doubled it with over 2,500 followers on Facebook, introducing new products: event promotion and social media marketing services!

As the number of clients increased, Ebony decided to publicize her business. With new opportunities coming up daily, she could see increased demand for digital advertising through sponsored posts on social media platforms like Facebook or Twitter. Her background in marketing has given her the skills to help small business owners sell more through online channels. As a leader, she dedicated her life to simplifying their tasks and achieving real results so they can focus on what's important - running the company!

A popular Cincinnati radio personality, Ms. Ebony J, can be heard playing fun dance music all around the greater Cincinnati tri-state area. From Women's Empowerment Events, Wedding Receptions, and Holiday Parties to Private engagements, Karaoke, and local night clubs to the concert stage, you'll find “The Queen” in her element when the dance floor is moving, and everyone is smiling & singing!

She has 14-plus years of media experience. She is a self-published author, a Syndicated Podcast Host, an Online Educator with Influence, and the owner of an award-winning business, Ebony J Media LLC, which helps businesses and brands curate content and events with unique strategies to increase brand awareness, engagement, and conversation rates.

Ms. Ebony J has written multiple media curriculums for colleges as well as high schools. She has launched successful campaigns for small businesses and corporations. She has coached over 1000 Brands and CEOs in the past 10 years.
