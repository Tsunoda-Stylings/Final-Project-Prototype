Jasmine Tsunoda June 2022 Client Highlights

FabulousYou LLC : Ashley Brogden

Tsunoda Stylings is a Japan-based agency that strives to create successful, responsive websites that are fast, easy to use, and built with the best techniques. At Tsunoda Stylings, we search for new and experienced small business owners looking to promote their businesses without breaking the bank! Not too long after we opened our doors, we received a request from a former home care provider that wanted a fresh and unique website for her newest business venture.

Please meet Ashley Brogden. She is the founder and CEO of FabulousYou LLC! She is talented, hard-working, passionate, and excited about giving women the spa treatments they need and deserve. Read more below to learn more about her and what she plans to accomplish with her new site designed by Tsunoda Stylings.

Interview with Ashley Brogden:

Jasmine: Tell me a little about your background. When and how did you start your business?

Ashley: I started off as a home care provider, and I did that for 8 years before starting my business. I obtained my LLC for FabulousYou LLC on 7/19/2021. I didn't know exactly what I wanted to do at that time. It was more of me wanting to walk into my fabulous. This was the beginning phase.

I was on Facebook one night and came across a woman's page named Jasmine Maria Gentry. She provided a certified Vaginal Steam Facilitator course, which also included a Foot Detox course. I passed the course and obtained my certification on March 8th, 2022. After my first certification, I came across Body Gamers Spa LLC. This was when I became interested in body contouring. I got certified in body sculpting and wood therapy, Vsteams, red light therapy, and more.

Everything went hand and hand with what I was learning to do. I was well on my way with my business plans, and I got certified on March 11th, 2022, for my online body contouring courses. I also attended John Academy for my Luxury Facial course and got the certification on March 30th.

Now all of my certifications make up FabulousYou LLC. I want everyone to walk in their fabulous and feel great about themselves with my help. I still have the passion for helping people as I always have, just in a different way.

Jasmine: Why did you decide to become a business owner?

Ashley: I became a business owner because I had two small children, and I was tired of clocking into a job. I want to be a stay-at-home mom and set my own hours. To have more time, money, and financial freedom for my family. It's always been a dream to have my own business and create a legacy.

Jasmine: How did you come up with the name for your company?

Ashley: I came up with the name FabulousYou LLC because I felt this was me walking into my fabulous part of my life, becoming a legal business owner.

Jasmine: What is your mission/company saying? How did you come up with that?

Ashley: My mission is to "Build Barbies One Woman At A Time The Natural Way And Start Walking In Your Fabulous Today." I just want everyone to feel good about themselves in their skin, and I want to help them with that. I want them to feel fabulous and comfortable without the pain of surgery.

Jasmine: How long did you study? Are there any other certifications you would like to get in the future?

Ashley: I studied from each course from home. One of the classes was one day for 8 hours, and I finished the test the following day. The facial class, however, took 4 weeks. The body contouring courses took 12 hours of non-stop courses, studying, and quizzes on a zoom chat. After I still had manuals to go over. But in the future, I also plan to take micro-blading, waxing, and lash courses.

Jasmine: What services or products do you offer?

Ashley: I offer Yoni Steams, Luxury Spa Facials, Wood Therapy, Red Light Therapy, Vaccum Therapy, Massages and Body Contouring.

Jasmine: Do you make any of the oils/products yourself? Do you want to in the future?

Ashley: At this time I have made an aloe and lime facial exfoliator. I also made ginger oil for your stomach with basic ingredients. I look forward to making more natural products in the future.

Jasmine: What is unique about your company?

Ashley: What makes my company unique is you can come to one place to get all of these services. I am passionate about each service that I provide.

Jasmine: What’s the best piece of advice anyone ever gave you in regards to your business?

Ashley: The best piece of advice in regards to my business is to trust the process and to give my clients the best results on their journey with all their natural body needs.

Jasmine: How did you find out about Tsunoda Stylings?

Ashley: I found out about Tsunoda Stylings from our brother Marc Knott. :)

Jasmine: Does your business give back to your community/charities?

Ashley: At this time I do not give back to the community or charities but I do plan to in the future as right now my business is still up and coming.

Jasmine: What are your goals for 2022?

Ashley: My goals for 2022 is to get my business out there for the world to see and just have a successful business!
